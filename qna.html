What is this app?
This app generates images based on user input using the Unsplash API.
Can I download images?
Yes, click the 'Download' button below each image to save it to your device.
Is the app free to use?
Yes, it is free to use, but it follows Unsplash's usage guidelines.
How many images can I generate at once?
You can generate up to 4 images at a time based on your search input.
What kind of images can I generate?
You can generate images for almost any concept, but the results depend on the availability of related photos on Unsplash.
What if no images are found?
If no images match your search query, you can try different keywords or check your internet connection.
Is my search history saved?
No, your search history is not saved. The app does not store any personal data.
Can I use the generated images commercially?
Images are sourced from Unsplash, so ensure you follow their licensing rules for commercial use.
What should I do if I encounter a bug?
If you encounter any issues, please contact the developer or check for updates to the app.
